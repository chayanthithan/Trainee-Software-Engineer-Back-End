Error - >
_____
Lombok Did not support to latest Version

solutions
_________

change the lombok version in pom.xml
1.18.30

		<dependency>
			<groupId>org.projectlombok</groupId>
			<artifactId>lombok</artifactId>
			<version>1.18.30</version>
			<scope>provided</scope>
		</dependency>
